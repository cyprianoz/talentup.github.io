We exist to empower talents in Africa.

TalentUp Africa is a social enterprise that will address the wide-spread skills and job mismatches in Sub-Saharan Africa. We work to empower Sub-Saharan African youth in providing them with digitized tools to showcase their talent and the relevant skills to be productive in the workforce, while offering placement services to both individuals and organizations.

Our Vision

Our vision is to solve Sub-Saharan Africa's crippling skills gaps.

Our Mission

Educate Africa's next generation of leaders.

Provide online distant education that prepares job-seeking candidates in Africa with the skills they need to find employment.

Provide digitized, objective, and time-efficient recruitment service to local and international clients in Africa.
