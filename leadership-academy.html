Welcome to Talentup Africa Leadership Academy

Empower yourself to Lead.

TalentUp Africa gives you the skills you need to become a productive employee and connects you with employers to find a job fast.

We draw on candidates’ background information and CV, complemented by assessment scores to identify Africa’s top talent and enroll them talent in six-week leadership program focusing on hard and soft skills development. Our programs are industry-specific, and we will work toward placing each graduate at an appropriate organization.

What TalentUp Africa offers you

In 6 weeks, we help you build your hard and soft skills to succeed as a leader. From the first day you join our Academy, you will be part of the TalentUp Africa family. We will be here to offer you support throughout your career.

Improve your talent

Our cutting-edge program helps you master hard and soft skills through a mixed-methods training methodology.

Find work quick

We work toward placing 100% of our graduates in fulltime positions 90 days after completing our program.

Enhance your leadership skills

We help you improve your leadership skills, build your self-confidence and team-work.

A family for life

You will form close bonds with your classmates through learning and growing together simultaneously as you will join an outstanding group of TalentUp Africa graduates.

Industry specific certifications
